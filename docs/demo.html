1. Click on the tagbar and start typing tags.
  2. To confirm a tag press SPACE, ENTER or click anywhere in the window (outside of the tagbar).
  3. Click an already existing tag to select it, then press DELETE or BACKSPACE to remove it.
  4. This demo has an autosuggestion list of the most common animals. Try to enter the letter 's' and then use UP / DOWN keys to select a suggestion. Hit ENTER to confirm.
